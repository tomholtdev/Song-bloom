Privacy Policy for Songbloom

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

Developer: Thomas Holt

Your Privacy Matters

Songbloom respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information or content.

What We Don’t Collect

  • No personal data (such as name, email, or location)
  • No user content is accessed or uploaded
  • No third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • No syncing to external servers

Your Content

All notes and voice recordings are stored locally on your device only. Songbloom cannot access, view, or recover any of your content.

Apple App Store Analytics

Apple may collect anonymous, aggregate analytics such as:

  • The country or region where the app was downloaded
  • General usage data and crash reports
  • Device type and OS version

This information is collected by Apple, not by Songbloom, and is used to help improve the app experience. You can manage your data-sharing preferences in your device settings.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through the App Store’s support contact.
